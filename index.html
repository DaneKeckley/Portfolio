ABOUT

Dane Keckley is a professional voice actor whose craft lies in transforming words into living, breathing characters. Known for his rich, versatile voice and refined control, Dane specializes in animation, delivering performances that range from heartfelt heroes to complex villains. Whether voicing a spirited protagonist or an otherworldly creature, Dane brings a cinematic sensibility and a nuanced understanding of tone and rhythm to every session.

Dane has built his career from the ground up and has landed himself in a multifaceted universe, playing diverging characters such as Yan Shu-Wen and the Dark One in The Bridge Curse 2, Gaia in Super Snail X, Patriot Sam in Enigma, Omus in Lone Soul, and William Afton and Michael Schmidt in The Return to Freddy’s.

In addition to his variety in vocal range, Dane has versatility in talent. He has made his mark in the modeling world, walking the Nordstrom runway and appearing in publications such as Vessel, Artells, Marika, and Top Posters. These experiences amplify his ability and have shaped his creative presence to embody character through subtlety and expression.

With a voice that carries warmth, power, and range, Dane Keckley continues to build a reputation as an accomplished performer who can adapt, elevate, and captivate. For casting directors and creators seeking a voice that can give their worlds dimension, Dane offers not just sound, but soul.
